Waste Sorting Tips

Plastic Bottles: Rinse them and place them in the plastic bin.
Glass: Only recycle clean glass bottles and jars (no ceramics).
Food Waste: Compost if possible. Do not mix with recyclables.
Paper/Tissues: Recycle clean paper. Tissues go into compost or trash depending on use.
